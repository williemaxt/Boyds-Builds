Payment Agreement

Payment is easy and afforable. We charge a $50 non-refundable deposit for creating the part list. Under this deposit you are entitaled to having us change the part list and working with you on figuring out what works and is best for your needs. After you accept the part list, we calculate the total cost of the parts, shipping, and labor and give you the total price. The cost of labor will always be the same low rate of 15% of the build total. The $50 deposit will be applied to the total cost so that you will not be paying anything extra. All of our price calculations are available upon requesting. Payment is due before the building process can begin.
